AP® Calculus: Challenging Concepts from Calculus AB & Calculus BC

Learn the most challenging topics in AP® Calculus AB and Calculus BC. Well-respected AP instructors from around the United States will lead you through video instruction, exam-style questions and interactive activities. Each module will cover one of the most demanding concepts in this AP® Calculus AB and Calculus BC (based on College Board data from 2011&emd;2013 Advanced Placement® exams). These tricky topics are broken up into bite&emd;sized pieces&emd;with short instructional videos, interactive graphs, and practice problems written by many of the same people who write and grade your AP® Calculus exams.

Topics

  1. AB/BC: Limits
  2. AB/BC: Definition of Derivative
  3. AB/BC: Chain Rule
  4. AB/BC: Implicit Differentiation
  5. BC*: L'Hospital's Rule
  6. AB/BC: Riemann Sums
  7. AB/BC: Functions Defined by Definite Integrals
  8. AB/BC: Modeling and Solving Differential Equations (1)
  9. AB/BC: Modeling and Solving Differential Equations (2)
  10. AB/BC: Rectilinear Motion
  11. BC: Parametric Equations
  12. BC: Introduction to Series
  13. BC: Series Convergence
  14. BC: Series Manipulation

About Davidson Next
